CRISTIANO RONALDO

CRISTIANO RONALDO (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes, the most by a European player. He has won 32 trophies in his career including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions Leagues, one UEFA European Championship and one UEFA Nations League.
